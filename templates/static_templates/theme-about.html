About McKinsey Academy

McKinsey Academy helps rising professionals advance their careers by providing distinctive learning experiences taught by distinguished practitioners on a global digital platform. McKinsey Academy empowers leaders and business professionals to build the skills, knowledge, and capabilities to enhance individual effectiveness for improved corporate performance.

McKinsey Academy prepares rising professionals for the next step in their careers through practitioner-led learning immediately applicable in the workplace.

How we work

McKinsey Academy courses are developed by distinguished practitioners and industry experts who share their insights and experience across key business topics. We use emerging learning tools and digital collaboration techniques to improve individual effectiveness and corporate performance.

  • Digital delivery using cutting-edge learning techniques
  • Created and convened by McKinsey partners from a practitioner’s perspective
  • Tailored for busy professionals
  • Collaborative and adaptive learning mechanics
  • Social learning with global, multi-industry cohorts
  • McKinsey teaching assistant guidance
  • Curated programs and individual courses
